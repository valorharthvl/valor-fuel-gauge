Privacy Policy

Valor AI Fuel Gauge — Chrome Extension
Last updated: April 11, 2026

Who We Are

Valor Hart LLC develops and operates the Valor AI Fuel Gauge Chrome extension. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you use our extension.

What Data We Collect

We are committed to collecting the absolute minimum data necessary to provide our service.

  • Anthropic API Key: Your API key is encrypted with AES-256-GCM and stored exclusively in Chrome's local storage on your device. It is never transmitted to our servers or any third party. The key is used only to make API calls directly from your browser to Anthropic's servers.
  • Token Usage Data: We track your token usage locally in Chrome storage to display your usage gauge. This data never leaves your device.
  • Action Pack Credit Balance: Your credit balance is stored locally in Chrome storage and updated when you make purchases or use actions.
  • Email Address: If you provide an email address in the settings page, it is stored locally in Chrome storage for account lookup purposes only.

What Data We Do Not Collect

We want to be explicitly clear about what we never collect:

  • We do not collect or access your browsing history
  • We do not read, store, or transmit any chat content from Claude or any other AI platform
  • We do not perform any DOM scraping of any website you visit
  • We do not collect personal information beyond what you explicitly provide
  • We do not use cookies or tracking pixels
  • We do not build user profiles or perform behavioral tracking

Payment Processing

All payments for Action Pack purchases are processed by Stripe, a PCI-compliant payment processor. When you purchase an Action Pack:

  • You are redirected to Stripe's secure hosted checkout page
  • All credit card details are entered directly on Stripe's servers
  • We never see, receive, or store your credit card number, expiration date, or CVV
  • Stripe provides us only with a confirmation that payment was successful and a session identifier for verification

Third-Party Services

Our extension interacts with the following third-party services:

  • Anthropic API (anthropic.com): Used to power AI summary actions. API calls are made directly from your browser using your own API key. Your selected text is sent to Anthropic for processing. Please review Anthropic's privacy policy for how they handle data sent to their API.
  • Stripe (stripe.com): Used for payment processing of Action Pack purchases. Please review Stripe's privacy policy.
  • Vercel (vercel.com): Hosts our backend API endpoints for creating checkout sessions and verifying payments. No user data is stored on Vercel servers.

Data Storage and Security

  • All sensitive data (API keys) is encrypted with AES-256-GCM using the Web Crypto API before storage
  • Encryption keys are generated per-installation and stored in Chrome's local storage
  • All data remains on your device in Chrome's local storage
  • No databases, servers, or cloud storage systems are used to store your personal data

No Data Selling

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise share your personal data with any third party under any circumstances. This is a core principle of our business and will not change.

Data Deletion

You can delete all data stored by the extension at any time by:

  • Using the Clear button on the Settings page to remove your API key
  • Uninstalling the extension, which removes all Chrome local storage data

Children's Privacy

Our extension is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
